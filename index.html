Day 1 (Friday): Arrival & Settle In
Road Trip
1:00 PM

Departure from Bellevue

Depart early Friday afternoon via I-90 E with your Model Y Long Range charged up to 80%. Enjoy the scenic ascent up Snoqualmie Pass.

2:15 PM

⚡ EV Charging & Stretch in Cle Elum (808 W Davis St)

Pull into the V3 Supercharger station. Take a 15-minute break to stretch, grab a quick drink at Gravity Coffee, and top your battery back up comfortably to cruise over to Chelan.

4:45 PM

Check-in & Quick Grocery Restock

Arrive at 255 Porcupine Lane to check into your vacation rental. Swing by Safeway or Chelan Market Fresh to pick up breakfast foods, milk, and snacks for the kids.

6:30 PM

Sunset Walk at Chelan Riverwalk Park

Head to 117 E Wapato Ave. Stretch your legs on a beautiful, flat 1-mile loop trail along the river. Great fields for the kids to run around on after the drive.

Day 2 (Saturday): Slidewaters Water Park
Full Day Adventure
9:30 AM

Pack the Beach Bags

Get the kids ready with sunblock, water shoes, and towels for a high-energy day at the park.

10:00 AM

Slidewaters (102 Waterslide Dr)

Spend the day enjoying the massive water park overlooking the lake. Features an incredible lazy river, safety-first splash zones for younger children, and thrilling tube slides. Rent a locker on-site or grab snacks at the concession stands.

5:30 PM

Dinner at Twisted Cork & Taphouse

Head downtown to 116 S Emerson St. After a big swimming day, enjoy premium Indian dishes like Tikka Masala or Samosas, while keeping things simple for the kids with their dedicated kids' menu selections.

Day 3 (Sunday): Relaxed Beach & Putting Greens
Easy-Going Family Day
10:30 AM

Don Morse Memorial Park (485 W Manson Hwy)

An easy, slow morning. Pack a picnic and head down to the premier sandy beach on the lake. The kids can swim in the protected shallow areas or enjoy the large playground structures.

2:00 PM

18-Hole Natural Putting Course

Located right inside Don Morse Park, play a relaxed round on the 18-hole grass putting course. It's scaled perfectly for children and incredibly low stress.

6:00 PM

Barbecue or Community Pool Chill

Head back up to Porcupine Lane. Take advantage of your home base to grill dinner or splash in the Lookout community neighborhood pool to wrap up the weekend.

Day 4 (Monday): Checkout & Drive Home
Heading Back to Bellevue
11:00 AM

Check-out of Rental Basecamp

Finish packing bags, load up the car, and check out of 255 Porcupine Lane right at 11:00 AM. Ensure your Model Y is prepped with an 80% charge baseline.

12:00 PM

Brunch or Early Lunch Downtown

Grab a comfortable meal at a local cafe or bakery in downtown Chelan before hitting the road.

3:15 PM

⚡ EV Top-up Stop in Cle Elum (808 W Davis St)

Stop over at the Cle Elum V3 supercharger again to give everyone a quick legs stretch, washroom break, and ensure you drop back down into Bellevue with tons of battery buffer.

5:00 PM

Arrival in Bellevue

Arrive home safely in Bellevue by 5:00 PM with plenty of evening left to unwind before the week starts.
