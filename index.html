Day 1: Arrival & Evening Stroll
Road Trip & Settle In
1:00 PM

Departure from Bellevue

Hit the road via I-90 E, passing through Snoqualmie Pass, and transitioning onto US-97 N. Enjoy a scenic, low-stress drive with the kids.

4:30 PM

Check-in & Grocery Run

Arrive at 255 Porcupine Lane. While stretching out and unpacking, take a quick 5-minute drive to Safeway or Chelan Market Fresh to stock up on snacks and weekend essentials.

6:30 PM

Chelan Riverwalk Park

Head to 117 E Wapato Ave. A flat, paved 1-mile loop trail along the water—perfect for letting the kids run around, stretch their legs, and enjoy the sunset scenery.

Day 2: Slidewaters Splash Day
Full Day Adventure
9:30 AM

Prep for the Waterpark

Lather on the first layer of sunscreen, pack your towels, water shoes, and change of clothes.

10:00 AM

Slidewaters (102 Waterslide Dr)

Arrive right at opening to grab a good spot! Experience a world-class water park featuring a lazy river, kid-friendly play zones, tube slides, and stunning views overlooking the lake. Concessions and lockers are available on-site.

5:00 PM

Return & Relax

Head back to the house to dry off, change, and unwind. Enjoy a casual family dinner at the house or walk around the community pool and pocket parks inside The Lookout.

Day 3: Lakeside Parks & Head Home
Beach Fun & Farewell
10:00 AM

Don Morse Memorial Park (485 W Manson Hwy)

Check out of your rental and head down to the lake. This premier park offers sandy beaches, fantastic playgrounds, clear swimming areas, and an 18-hole natural turf putting course that's fun for all ages.

12:30 PM

Downtown Lunch

Grab a relaxed family bite or pick up deli sandwiches in downtown Chelan before hitting the road.

2:00 PM

Drive back to Bellevue

Head southwest back over the mountain passes, ensuring a smooth ride home.

5:00 PM

Arrive in Bellevue

Arrive home in Bellevue with wonderful memories and plenty of time to unpack before the night.
