🧺 Toddler-Friendly Picnic Parks

Shade Trees & Playground

Don Morse Memorial Park

Spanning 40 waterfront acres, it is perfect for small kids. It offers a dedicated children's playground, massive open lawns with shade trees, and a protected, shallow swimming zone.

Amenities: Kiosk parking, restrooms, sandy zero-entry beach lines.

Quieter Beach Setting

Lakeside Park

A beautiful 10-acre neighborhood park located in the historic Lakeside community. Features expansive grassy lawns under beautiful mature shade trees, keeping toddlers protected from high midday heat.

Amenities: Playground, restrooms, picnic tables, and clear shallow swimming bays.

Manson Waterfront

Manson Bay Park

Located just up the road in downtown Manson. Features an expansive lush green lawn hitting the waterfront dock perimeter, pristine benches, and immediate access to toddler walking lines.

Amenities: Playground across the street, beach safety lines, nearby cafes.

🛶 Boat, Kayak, & Jet Ski Rentals

South Shore Base

Lake Shores Watercraft Rentals

Highly rated family outfit located close to Slidewaters. Perfect for a streamlined logistics day out on the water.

Fleet: Powerboats, Jet Skis, Paddleboards, Single/Double Kayaks

Downtown Fleet

Shoreline Watercraft

Boasts one of the largest premier rental fleets located directly within the marina hub. Turn-key boats ready to go.

Fleet: Pontoons, Luxury open-bows, Starcraft deck boats

Parasail & Speed

Chelan Parasail & Watercraft

Over 30 years of local service. A fantastic choice for speedboats, waverunners, or high-flying parasailing options.

Fleet: Ski Boats, WaveRunners, Parasail packages

🎡 Extra Family Fun Activities

Scenic Day Trip

Lady of the Lake Ferry Tour

Take a stunning passenger ferry cruise deep into the 55-mile fjord where roads can't reach.

Activity: Cruising and day-tripping up to remote Stehekin.

Historic Fun

The Ruby Theatre

The oldest operating cinema in WA (1914) featuring a grand character design. Great for hot afternoons.

Activity: Catching a relaxed family movie layout.

High Ropes

High Trek Chelan

Located right adjacent to the Slidewaters complex, this keeps family members active up in the air.

Activity: Aerial obstacle climbing courses and ziplines.

🍕 Family Restaurants For After The Fun

Manson Farm-to-Table

Blueberry Hills Farm

A welcoming 20-acre scratch-kitchen experience. Ranked as a top destination layout in Washington State.

Food: Famous yeast waffles, massive burgers, fresh scratch fruit pies.

Downtown Craft Pizza

Local Myth Pizza

A local institution serving fresh-tossed artisanal pies, signature kids' builds, and custom giant calzones.

Food: Craft pizzas, gluten-free crust options, artisan dips.

Scratch Americana

County Line Chelan

A casual restaurant right on Woodin Ave serving locally sourced high-quality items on a family patio setup.

Food: Street tacos, gourmet handheld sandwiches, fresh salads.
